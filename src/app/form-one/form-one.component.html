Here are a few tips:

Your Bio will be 3 sentences long and will appear in your header profile. This is the first piece of information that people will see on your CV and Profile- so make it as clear and concise as you can.
  • Mention the stage you’re at in your career/ education. Here are some ideas: Year 10 student, college student, Year 11 school leaver, etc.
  • Now, add your favourite subject, skill or achievement which is relevant to the job or experience you would like.
  • Finally, you need to pinpoint which type of opportunity you want. Maybe your looking for a mentor? Or for a work experience placement? A part or full-time job maybe? Even an apprenticeship...
This text will appear in the header of your profile, and also at the top of your CV.
